Pottery

The pottery studio at ENTS is fully equipped for hand building or wheel throwing. Members are welcome to bring their own clay and glazes. Materials are not mandated by the space.

We encourage contributing to a communal glaze library - potluck style!

Bisque and glaze firings happen roughly twice a week - this is completed by volunteer members
