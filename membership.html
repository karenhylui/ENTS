Safety

ENTS is an active workspace: closed toe shoes and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) are highly recommended. Masks are recommended, but not required.

PPE is provided throughout the space. Members are welcome to bring their own PPE. If any PPE is missing or damaged, please contact our team and it will be repaired or replaced.
